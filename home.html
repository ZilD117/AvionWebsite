Mission

Pioneering the Future of Global Weather Forecast with AI
Who we are

An AI-Powered Weather Forecasting Pioneer.

In 2023, our founders — a team of interdisciplinary experts with backgrounds in physics, computer science, and atmospheric sciences — were inspired to revolutionize weather forecasting after witnessing the limitations of traditional methods. Instead of advanced, cutting-edge technology, they found outdated models struggling to keep up with the demands of accurate and timely predictions. Recognizing the critical need for innovation, they embarked on a mission to harness the power of AI and advanced physics to transform weather forecasting.

Impact

Driving Innovation and Excellence in Weather Forecasting.

Since our inception, we have partnered with leading research institutions and industry experts to push the boundaries of what is possible in weather prediction. Our innovative solutions have been instrumental in reducing operational costs, enhancing safety, and improving decision-making across various sectors. By continuously refining our models and expanding our data sources, we strive to set new standards in the field of weather forecasting.
